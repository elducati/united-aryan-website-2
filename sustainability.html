How We Make a Difference

Go-Green Initiatives

Investing in latest sustainable technology — LESS water, chemicals, energy and discharge in Washing & Dry Process using Laser Technology to produce 120,000 units a day.

Use of certified green chemicals and ZHDC-certified chemical inputs across all production processes.

Installing an Effluent Treatment Plant with capacity of 2,000,000 litres/day — fully recycling treated water.

1.8 Mega Watts of Solar power — equivalent to saving 33,000 tons of carbon and 65,000 trees over its lifetime.

Increased use of recycled raw materials throughout production.

Medical & Health Benefits

Arranging comprehensive medical and life insurance coverage for all our workers and their immediate families.

Running health awareness programmes including HIV Sensitization, early detection tests for cervical and breast cancer, eye testing, and gynecological screening.

On-site medical facilities to ensure quick access to healthcare for our entire workforce.

Community Support

Supporting local schools, orphanages and old-age homes in our neighbourhood — contributing to the social fabric of the community.

Extending sponsorship to underprivileged bright students in our community, investing in the next generation of leaders.

Supporting a football team of talented players from our workforce — who have already won the 2nd Division League and are now part of the national Super League.

Networking & Collaboration

We partner with government and non-government organisations that enhance the wellbeing of our surrounding community:

St. Joseph Catholic Church, Mulolongo — offering technical training to local unemployed youth.

Shadrack Juma Foundation, Baba Dogo — training women and single mothers in the art of garment making.

Kenya Women Children Wellness Centre — eliminating gender-based violence and building entrepreneurship skills for financial liberation.
